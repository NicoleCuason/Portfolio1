Singapore Travel Poster



Graphic Design


This travel poster for Singapore, created in Adobe Illustrator, blends modern innovation and cultural heritage to spark curiosity and inspire exploration.


Project Overview


I created a travel poster for Singapore, highlighting landmarks like Marina Bay Sands, the ArtScience Museum, and the Merlion. The design emphasizes the city’s modern architecture and cultural heritage through a clean, balanced layout. For this poster, I chose Singapore and focused on its iconic landmarks: Marina Bay Sands, the ArtScience Museum, and the Merlion. I refined high-quality images in Adobe Illustrator, adjusting lighting and colors to highlight each landmark while maintaining a balanced composition. I arranged the images thoughtfully to guide the viewer’s eye and used alignment principles for visual harmony. Finally, I added carefully selected text, ensuring the typography complemented the design without overpowering the visuals.
